Superstore Sales Analysis

Sales Distribution Across States

The top three states with the highest sales are California, New York, and Texas, while the states with the lowest sales are Wyoming, Vermont, and North Dakota. This concentration indicates that regions with large urban populations tend to have higher sales volumes.

Category Sales Comparison

Technology leads in sales, likely due to high-value items, while Office Supplies shows the lowest sales, possibly because of lower purchase frequency.

Monthly Sales Trend

The monthly sales trend shows peaks in specific months, indicating possible seasonal patterns. Peak sales occur in months like November and December, possibly due to holiday shopping. Consistent patterns may highlight periods of increased consumer spending.

Profit vs. Sales Analysis

The scatter plot reveals that certain sub-categories, such as Tables, have high sales but lower profit margins, while others like Phones have both high sales and high profit. This could guide the company in promoting high-profit items more Frequently .

Sales by Shipping Mode

Standard Class shipping generates the highest sales, while Same Day has the lowest. This information suggests that customers are more likely to choose cost-effective shipping options, which could help the company optimize logistics costs by focusing on popular shipping modes.
